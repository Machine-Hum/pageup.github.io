DiscreetPaging

Overview

Pageup Discreet allows your kitchen notify servers the instant food or drinks are ready to be picked up. All of the servers names are listed on an Android tablet, after kitchen staff selects the desired server, they are notified by a custom vibration - directly to their phone. This makes for fast and efficient communication between the servers, kitchen and bar. Multiple tablets can be stationed at kitchen, bar and concierge.



Advantages

  • Customers are not disturbed by noisy kitchen bells

  • Staff have more time to spend with the customers

  • Throughput of the restaurant is increased

  • Customers are served more efficiently

  • Food never gets cold

Details

  • Compatible with any Android Device

  • No data plan required

  • Stable App
