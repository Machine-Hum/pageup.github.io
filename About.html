WP Tech is a company formed by two programmers, thinkers, inventors and dreamers… Ryan Walker & Rashesh Patel.

What We Do

PageUP started when me and Rashesh were taking a walk during a power outage at work, Rashesh had the idea from his experience in the restaurant industry. Initially we were excited but quickly realized that we were not the only people with the same idea.

At first we we’re upset, but when we started looking through existing products we noticed most were cheaply made plastic disks with stickers for labels. “Who would want that junky thing in their restaurant” Rashesh said. After working in the food industry for over ten years he knows that presentation is everything - That was our jumping off point.

We bring you something gorgeous, strong and functional.
